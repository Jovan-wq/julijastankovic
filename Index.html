O Meni

Hi, I’m Julija

An illustrator and graphic designer drawn to quiet stories, soft colors, and delicate details. My creative world is built around hand-drawn elements, pastel tones, and a sense of calm. Whether I’m working on book covers, invitations, or visual identities, I always aim to create something that feels personal, poetic, and thoughtfully crafted.

I’m inspired by nature, childhood memories, and the little things that often go unnoticed. My process often begins with a sketchbook and a pencil, where each line is a gentle exploration. From there, I carefully build compositions that are both visually soothing and emotionally resonant.

Design, for me, is a way of telling stories — not loudly, but with warmth and honesty
